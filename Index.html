Latest Release: "For Her"

In the wreckage of a tragic car accident, Alex Wise loses his wife, Natalie, and young daughter, Arlie—shattering his world and drowning him in grief and guilt. Three years later, he encounters Connie, a blind survivor of the same crash, whose haunting connection to his past reignites a flicker of hope. With his sister Camila's steadfast support, Alex confronts his demons and rebuilds amid unbearable pain. As his bond with Connie deepens, they navigate shared scars toward healing—until Alex makes the ultimate sacrifice to save her life. FOR HER is a heartfelt testament to resilience, forgiveness, and love's enduring power, proving that even in profound loss, redemption awaits.

Excerpt from "For Her"

She blew, felt the heat disappear, then return.

"Blow," he said again. She blew, not sure if he was teasing her, but she didn't mind.

He sat her upright, then she heard the sound of forks and a plate. "Alright, I have something for you. Open wide."

She opened her mouth, tasting chocolate cake. "Happy birthday, Connie Kurt."
